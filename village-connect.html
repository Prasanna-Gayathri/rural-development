Services We Offer

Medicine Delivery

We provide timely delivery of essential medicines to rural areas. Whether it's a common cold medicine or a prescription for chronic conditions, we ensure that medical supplies reach you directly at your doorstep.

Doctor Home Visits

Our platform helps you book a doctor for home visits. Whether it's a routine check-up or an emergency, the doctor will visit your home at your preferred time and date, ensuring the best care at your convenience.

Plumbing Services

Need a plumber to fix a leak or install new fixtures? Our skilled plumbing service providers are just a click away, ready to help with all plumbing-related issues at your home or farm.

Electrician Services

Electrical problems? No worries! Book an electrician through our platform, and we'll connect you with an expert to handle any electrical issues, from wiring to lighting setup.

Carpentry Services

Our carpenters can assist you with furniture making, repairs, and other woodwork-related services. Book a carpenter today to make your home functional and stylish.

How It Works

Follow these simple steps to request a service:

  1. Select the service you need (medicine delivery, doctor home visit, plumbing, etc.).
  2. Fill in the service request form with your details.
  3. Confirm the service details, including date and time.
  4. Wait for confirmation and enjoy the service at your doorsteps.
